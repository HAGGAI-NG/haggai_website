Why HAGGAI?

As the Lord instructed the prophet HAGGAI in Haggai 1:3-8

"Then came the word of the Lord by Haggai the prophet, saying, Is it time for you, O ye, to dwell in your ceiled houses, and this house lie waste? Now therefore thus saith the Lord of hosts; Consider your ways. Ye have sown much, and bring in little; ye eat, but ye have not enough; ye drink, but ye are not filled with drink; ye clothe you, but there is none warm; and he that earneth wages earneth wages to put it into a bag with holes. Thus saith the Lord of hosts; Consider your ways. Go up to the mountain, and bring wood, and build the house; and I will take pleasure in it, and I will be glorified, saith the Lord."

and according to 1 Corinthians 6:19, our bodies are God's temple.

"What? know ye not that your body is the temple of the Holy Ghost which is in you, which ye have of God, and ye are not your own?"

As a result, we created Haggai to assist in fulfilling the Lord's instruction to build, as the best way to develop the Temple would be through the Lord's word (2 Timothy 2:15)

"Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth."

OUR MISSION IS TO ASSIST IN THE BUILDING OF THE TEMPLE, AND WE DREAM OF A DAY WHEN ALL MEN WILL BE COMPLETELY BUILT ON CHRIST.

Rccg Vision and mission

As received by the General Overseer (G.O.), Pastor E. A. Adeboye, and communicated to the Headquarters leaders, the church vision and mission statement shall remain intact. They are as follows:

1.

To make heaven.

2.

To take as many people with us.

3.

To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.

4.

To accomplish No. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.

5.

To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.

6.

We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ
